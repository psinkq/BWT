Bombsite B

Bombsite B is located to the right of the Counter-Terrorist Spawn and is situated around an obelisk. Terrorists have two entrances - one through B main and the second through B connector (or sometimes known as Secret). Counter-Terrorists have two primary pathways to retake the bombsite from. These both approach the bombsite from a similar direction. Counter-Terrorists can still retake the bombsite through the terrorist entrances by either flanking through their spawn or from mid.
