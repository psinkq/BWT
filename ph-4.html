Water or canal

Anubis’s water, or canal, as it’s sometimes called, is a narrow waterway that runs alongside the map’s central area. It’s a flanking route for both attackers and defenders, and while it offers some cover, it’s also incredibly exposed and vulnerable. Attackers might use it to try and reach the bomb sites from an unexpected angle, or to disrupt defenders from behind. Defenders can use the water to rotate quickly between bomb sites, or to try and cut off attackers who are attempting to flank. However, the water itself offers very little cover, and players are vulnerable to being picked off by defenders or attackers, making it a risky proposition. The tight spaces make it easy to get cornered and vulnerable, especially when using a throwable.
