Bombsite A

Bombsite A is located to the left of Counter-Terrorist Spawn and is set between around a cylindrical object. It has one entrance directly from Terrorist spawn (known as A Main), and another entrance for the terrorists which links through mid. The corridor feeds to a walkway above the bombsite, where Terrorists can easily get control of Heaven and the entirety of the bombsite. Counter-Terrorists can hold the A bombsite from places like Heaven, on the Bombsite, A Connector. If they want a more aggressive stance, they can opt for a position like Plat or close to A Main.
