Middle

Anubis Mid is a crucial area, characterized by its tight chokepoints and multiple flanking routes. Control of mid grants access to both bomb sites, making it a high-value target.


Key Features:

    1. Chokepoint: The narrow passage leading to the bomb sites is a natural chokepoint, where defenders can hold off attackers with ease.
    2. Flanking routes: Both sides of mid offer flanking opportunities, allowing attackers to outmaneuver defenders or defenders to counter-push.
    3. Sniper nest: The top of the mid-control building provides a strategic vantage point for snipers, allowing them to control the area and provide cover fire.
    4. Verticality: The mid-control building offers a significant height advantage, giving players a view of the entire area.


Strategies:

Attackers:
  • Utilize smoke grenades to obscure defenders’ vision and push through the chokepoint.
  • Prioritize flanking routes to create confusion and disrupt enemy positioning.
  • Use grenades to clear corners and deny enemy access to high ground.
Defenders:
  • Utilize the chokepoint to hold off attackers with ease.
  • Position players on both sides of mid to counter-push attackers.
  • Utilize the high ground to provide cover fire and control the area.
Remember: Mid-control is a highly contested area, so it’s important to coordinate with your team and communicate effectively. Understanding the chokepoints, flanking routes, and verticality of the area is key to success.
